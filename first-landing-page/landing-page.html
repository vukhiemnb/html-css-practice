Yudu

A minimal & smooth to-do app where you can add tasks from anywhere on the internet.

Quick Start:

Just a regular todo list but focuses on good UI/UX and Minimalism.

1] Select/Highlight any text in email, messages, etc

2] Click Yudu chrome extension

3] It will automatically add tasks on your Yudu account

4] Continue with your day and check Yudu later.

All we requrie is your email address. We don't share it.

By continuing, you agree to terms and policies.

Here's why I like Yudu:

Just email@address to start using

Right click task to access options

No circle/checkbox. Just click the task to check off

Very simple & smooth animations

Group similar tasks and switch easily between them.

Add frequently visited URL in task groups
